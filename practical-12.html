Shiddat

Director: Kunal Deshmukh

Release Date: October 1, 2021

Genre: Romance/Drama

Movie Synopsis

A passionate love story involving two couples which highlights the contrast between a regular, mature yet egoistic husband and a unique, happy go lucky, enamored, overzealous boy chasing after the love of his life. After falling madly in love, a man tries to convey his feelings. However, the object of his affections is betrothed to another and asks him to prove his love.

Starring: Radhika Madan, Sunny Kushal, Diana Penty, Mohit Raina
